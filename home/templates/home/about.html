About Us

Rajputs Del Mundo literally translates to "Warriors Of The World." Read below to see our story.

About Rajputs Del Mundo

Our 11 a side team name was inspired by the owners, the fact that our team is so multicultural and includes players from many nationalities and ethnicities. We aim to bring the people of the world together and create unity through football but also give a chance for everyone to play the game they love competitively and be part of something special.

We have players from England, France, the Carribean, Nigeria, Senegal, Poland, Serbia, Iraq, India, Pakistan, Fiji Islands, Saudi Arabia, Somalia, Algeria, Morocco, Iran, Australia, Indonesia, Portugal, Spain, Uganda, Cyprus, Colombia, Ivory Coast, Lebanon, Japan, Ghana...just to name a few!!!!

Love for Football

Our love for the game is unparalleled and we have 7 managers/coaches and 4 teams who have played for years at different levels and clubs across about 80 registered players but ultimately our players are what makes this club so unique and we share a friendship circle across the teams.

We also hold tournaments during the summer to get more players involved in the game and keep our teams fit for the upcoming season!
